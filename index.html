WELCOME TO FUZZYVIL!



Meet the Mayor!

The mayor of Fuzzyvil is Oswald Fuzzybuns. He is always helping around town in his iconic hero's clothes and beak with his assistant, Isabelle. If you ever need help, Mayor Oswald will be sure to help!


About Fuzzyvil

Fuzzyvil is a stunning town with a beautiful view of the ocean. It is close to Main Street, which is full of shops, services, and even the Fuzzyvil Museum. Fuzzyvil makes use of the beautiful town ordinance, so you must pick up after yourself in attempt to keep Fuzzyvil beautiful. The town is complete with a retail store, town hall, coffee shop, police station, town plaza, and multiple other projects. Any visitors will be welcomed with open arms from our friendly villagers.

Thinking of Moving?

Our town offers a limit of 10 residents at a time. To move, please inform Tom Nook, and he will help you find a location to build your house. However, there are limits as to where you can build your house. You can reach Fuzzyvil by train. There, Porter the Monkey will direct you to Tom Nook's Homes.
